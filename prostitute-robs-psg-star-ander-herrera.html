Prostitute Robs PSG Star Ander Herrera

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

A prostitute has reportedly robbed Spanish professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain, Ander Herrera.

It was gathered that the commercial sex worker forced herself into the player’s car on Tuesday night and zoomed off with his wallet and mobile phone.

Naija News learnt that Herrera was patiently waiting at a red light in the Bois de Boulogne public park, located on the west side of Paris when the lady opened his car and hurriedly carried out her mission.

A report by Le Parisien claimed that the 32-year-old player was later directed to drive to a specific location to retrieve his stolen phone.

According to the report, police were reached at 8 pm that night in regards to the incident. Naija News understands that Herrera’s wallet contained 200 euros (£169).

The police have said that the PSG player reserved the right to file a complaint.

In another news, the Federation Internationale de Football Association, FIFA has handed a two-year ban to a Nigerian football coach, Remi Amadi.

Naija News reports that the football governing body restricted the coach of Cofine FC of Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, from taking part in all football-related activities until the ban is lifted.

The Nigerian Football Federation has been notified of Amadi’s suspension and the disciplinary action is said to be a similar ban imposed on the gaffer by the NFF Disciplinary Committee at its meeting of 16th August 2021.
