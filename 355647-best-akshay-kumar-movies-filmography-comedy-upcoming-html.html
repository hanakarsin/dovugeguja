Best Akshay Kumar movies: Filmography, comedy, upcoming

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Sunday, February 18, 2024

Akshay Kumar movies have made him the king of the box office in Bollywood for more than two decades. He has done quite a number of films in the industry, and every film of his has turned out to be a major hit. His films have not only broken the record barriers but have also ruled millions of hearts.

Akshay Kumar, whose real name is Rajiv Hari Om Bhatia, wears many hats. He is an actor, producer, television personality, and martial artist. Akshay made his debut in the film industry in the 1980s when he was given a minor role in the Indian film Aaj, which was directed by Mahesh Bhatt. Since then, he has been cast to play a lead role in many Bollywood films. His career spans more than two decades.
