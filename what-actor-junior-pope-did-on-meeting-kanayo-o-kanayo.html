What Actor Junior Pope Did On Meeting Kanayo O. Kanayo

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Popular Nollywood actor, Junior Pope Odonwodo on Thursday prostrated flat on the floor after meeting with his senior colleague Kanayo O. Kanayo.

Naija News reports that the father of three honored his senior colleague, Kanayo whom he described as a legend.

In the video that surfaced online captured the moment the actor ran to Kanayo and prostrated before him.

Sharing the video via his verified Instagram handle, Junior Pope talked about how legends are made from iron and sweat, mind and muscle, blood and vision, before victory.

According to Junior Pope, legends are champions who grow, win, and conquer. He said that there’s a legend behind every legacy and a blueprint behind every legend.

“Legends are made from iron & sweat, mind, and muscle, blood and vision and victory. Legends are champions, they grow, they win, they conquer. There’s a legend behind every legacy, there’s a blueprint behind every legend. @kanayo.kanayo IS A LEGEND… Say no more,” he captioned the Instagram video.
