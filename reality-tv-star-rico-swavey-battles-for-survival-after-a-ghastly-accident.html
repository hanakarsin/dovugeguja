Reality TV Star, Rico Swavey, Battles For Survival After A Ghastly Accident

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, March 2, 2024

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate, Rico Swavey, is currently battling for life after being involved in a ghastly accident.

Naija News reports that this was made known on Tuesday in a post shared on Twitter by his colleague, Alex Unusual as she calls for prayers.

According to Alex, the reality TV star was involved in an accident a few hours ago and the doctors are still experiencing difficulty in trying to resuscitate him at the hospital.

She tweeted: “Good morning sweethearts. Please put @iamricoswavey in your prayers today. He was involved in a ghastly accident hours ago and the doctors are still trying to resuscitate him as I just left the hospital. It’s really critical. I’m staying positive.#PrayForRicoSwavey”

DJ Big N Knocks MC Oluomo

Disco Jockey, Nonso Ajufo better known as DJ Big, has slammed the Chairman of Lagos State Parks and Garages management Committee, Musiliu Akinsanya, popularly known as MC Oluomo, over his comment on the Lekki shooting.

Naija News earlier reported that MC Oluomo had on Sunday claimed that nobody was killed during the shooting at the Lekki TollGate on October 20, 2020.

He said this during a rally he organized in Lagos in support of the All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu.

MC Oluomo asserted that the Lekki shooting could not be linked to the former Governor of Lagos State, adding that the #EndSARS protest was used to smear Tinubu’s name.

Reacting to the development, DJ Big N via his Instagram page described Oluomo’s remarks as insensitive and bland.

He also added that people with families died during the shooting.

He wrote; ‘‘Very delicate and insensitive time to talk about such. It Is almost two years after the mayhem that happened in the toll gate.”
