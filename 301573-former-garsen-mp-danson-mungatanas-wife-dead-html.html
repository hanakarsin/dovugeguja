Former Garsen MP Danson Mungatana's wife is dead

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, July 29, 2024

- Violet Mungatana passed on at the Coptic Hospital in Nairobi

- She was reportedly admitted at the hospital in February 2019

- It was, however, not immediately clear what she was suffering from

Former Garsen Member of Parliament Danson Mugatana's wife, Violet, has passed on at a Nairobi hospital.

The first wife to the outspoken politician died at the Coptic Hospital where TUKO.co.ke understands she was undergoing dialysis.

The sad news was shared by Deputy President William Ruto who led Kenyans in condoling with Mungatana's family.

"Condolences to family and friends of former Garsen MP Danson Mungatana following the death of his wife, Violet Mungatana. She was a devoted family woman; kind and welcoming, who stood out for her generosity and warm heart. Rest In Peace," the DP tweeted on Monday, March 25, evening.

Violet was reportedly admitted at the the Coptic Hospital in February 2019 and has been undergoing treatment at the said facility ever since.

It was, however, not immediately clear what she was suffering from.

Violet was one of Mungatana's three wives, the others being former Miss Kenya Cecilia Mwangi, who has since parted ways with the polygamous politician, and K24 news anchor Mwanaisha Chidzuga who is still married to the ex-MP.

It is understood Mungatana had three children with his late first wife best known by relatives and friends as Mama Elsie and who was a lawyer like her husband.
