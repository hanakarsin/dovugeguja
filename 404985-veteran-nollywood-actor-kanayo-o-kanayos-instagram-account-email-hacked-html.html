- In a video shared via his Twitter page, the 59-year-old charged his followers to disregard any message from the account at this time

In a 36-second video, the actor charged his fans to disregard any message coming from his Instagram account as he revealed and assured fans that his team is working on recovering the accounts.

He said in the video:

“I’m making this video to inform you all that my Instagram account has been hacked and my email also. Please disregard every information or message coming to you at this moment. It’s not made by me. Please help to repost this message. We will get rid of these guys no matter how many steps they get ahead.”
