Billionaire businessman Prince Arthur Ezes nephew dedicates first son (photos)

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

Politician and nephew of billionaire businessman Prince Arthur Eze's nephew, Olisa Eze and his beautiful wife recently held a church dedication for their first son.

Prince Olisa Eze and wife who are already parents to two lovely daughters, took their first son to church on Sunday, July 16, 2017, to dedicate him to God.

The cute little boy was christened Chimdindu Olisa Eze, at the St. Matthew's Anglican Communion in Maitama, Abuja.

The dedication was very colourful and also attracted lots of dignitaries who came to support the Eze family.
