Fans Amused As Chioma Akpotha Serves As Hypewoman To 'Oyibo' Man

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

Nollywood actress Chioma Akpotha has gotten her fans talking with the video of a foreigner dancing with masquerades.

The Nollywood actress shared a video of a white guy, Jerome Weber, dancing with masquerades on a movie set in what seemed like a happy situation while Chioma praised him for his unskilled dance moves.

Chioma explained the meaning of culture in her post with a caption that says: “I LOVE HOW @JEROMEWEBER JUMPED IN AND STARTED TO GROOVE WITH THE DANCE TROOP. NEVER BEEN TO NIGERIA BEFORE AND HE ABSOLUTELY LOVES IT HERE. LOVES THE FOOD, DRINKS AND MUSIC. MORE SO HE IS CONVINCED I’M THE BEST HYPEMAN EVER.
