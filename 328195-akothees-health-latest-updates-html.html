Akothee's health: Latest updates - Tuko.co.ke

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, March 17, 2024

Popular musician Esther Akoth popularly known by her stage name Akothee collapsed while performing during the Luo Festival in Kisumu on Saturday. Ever since then, her fans have been wondering what’s Akothee’s health status. Is she fine? What made her faint?

One of the most celebrated yet controversial musicians, Akothee, was hyping her audience during the Luo Festival when she collapsed on stage. Though the dancers caught her before she could hit the ground, the audience was in utter shock.

Akothee’s health update

Here is an update on how it all went down now and how she is at the moment:

Akothee collapses on stage

On the 30th of November 2019, celebrated singer Akothee was among the performers who graced the prestigious Luo Festival that took place in Kisumu. From the video clip that has made rounds all over the various social media platforms, the controversial musician is seen engaging her fans in a vigorous dance.

She was performing her latest hit song dubbed Rollam before she fell backwards on the stage. The incidence was so sudden and unanticipated that it caught a majority of the audience off-guard. Many of them were seen looking utterly surprised when they found her lying on the ground.

Instant first aid

Immediately after the incident, her dancer, together with her security team, took her off the stage and went to offer first aid. She had successfully run her 45 minutes set only to collapse during the final 30 seconds of the song. The fainting was caused by the vigorous up and down activities that made her lose her breath.

Oaks and the dancers didn’t have to go all the way to the hospital. Instead, they took her into the car for first aid. She was given a glass of juice and vitamins, which helped her to recover slowly into the ordinary self. Although she has been resting at the Acacia Hotel, she will soon be headed to her Rongo Spa Home as the doctors recommended she takes a bed rest for two weeks.

Akothee admitted to hospital

Two days before the incident, Akothee had just been discharged from Karen hospital, where she had been admitted and diagnosed with flu and fatigue. This took place a few days after arriving from Dubai.

She went ahead to assure her loyal fans that she was fine as well as warning the bloggers from creating exaggerated stories from her health condition. She shared a photo of her in a hospital bed and here is what she captioned;

This life, you can only occupy one bed at a time, bloggers don’t scare my family and fans. I am not dead! My system just shut down. I will be back.

Akothee health update and explanation from her manager

With her huge fan base, people have been eager to find out Akothee’s latest health update and what could have caused the collapse. Fortunately, her able manager Mr Nelly Oaks came to the fans rescue to offer them an explanation of why she fainted.

Akothee had engaged in heavy activity even after the doctors suggesting that she should take a rest. The mother of five checked out of the hospital and went straight to rehearse for the festival because she did not want her fans to get disappointed.

Response from fans

Fans have gone ahead to express their opinion and feelings after the collapsing of Akothee at Luo festival. While some thought that it was genuine and wished her quick recovery, others believed that the entire incidence was stage-managed. Here is what some of her fans had to say.

We love you more. Please take some rest, said Achieng.How? Kwani the dancers were aware that she was going to collapse, wondered Ann.The dancers were waiting for her to collapse! This was purely stage-managed. The question is? What does she want to achieve, questioned Marie.

Akothee has remained the majority’s favourite over the years. Although the recent incidence during the Luo festival in Kisumu left many astonished, the latest updates on Akothee’s health is quite a relief. We wish her a quick recovery.
