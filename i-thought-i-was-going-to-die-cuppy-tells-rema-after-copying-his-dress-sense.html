I Thought I Was Going To Die

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, January 1, 0001

Popular Nigerian Disc Jockey and billionaire daughter Florence Otedola popularly known as DJ Cuppy has revealed that she almost died after rocking a balaclava to one of her DJing gigs at Oxford University.

According to Cuppy, she decided to try the fashion accessory after she failed to glam up for the Djing gig. She shared videos and photos of herself rocking the pink balaclava and a pink coat.

Tagging Rema in one of the videos she posted, she asked how he is able to rock his balaclava comfortably.

Cuppy explained that she thought she was going to die of heat because she found it difficult to breathe while putting on the mask.

Meanwhile, Nollywood actress cum philanthropist, Tonto Dikeh has threatened controversial investigative journalist, Kemi Olunloyo as she storms Lagos to arrest her.

Kemi Olunloyo and Tonto Dikeh traded words on social a few days ago where the former alleged that the mother of one bought a gun to kill her ex-husband Olakunle Churchill and consumes hard drugs.

Tonto Dikeh urged Kemi Olunloyo to provide proof and name the list of friends who probably gave her the hint that she smokes.
