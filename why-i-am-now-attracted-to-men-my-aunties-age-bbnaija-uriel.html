'Why I Am Now Attracted To Men My Aunties' Age

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, April 26, 2024

Former Big Brother Naija housemate, Uriel Oputa has taken to her Instagram story to talk about how aging is making her settle for men she felt less attracted to when she was younger.

According to the reality star who is currently in search of a life partner, she is being pushed to like older men due to the way men in her age group treat her.

The UK-based chef noted that she is left with no choice but to go for men way older than her since the younger men do not treat women her age properly.

Taking to her Instastories on Monday, she wrote: ”It’s funny I never used to be attracted to older (single guys) but now the guys my age rage 30s are showing us pepper back to back.. let me go and ask my aunties if their classmates are single. Everyday pepper pepper ah ah My guys cool o down.”

Uriel had in previous posts narrated how her ex-partner made her feel insecure.

In a post shared on her Instagram stories on Uriel Oputa mentioned that her former partner made her feel insecure about her body.

“I remember my ex said my legs weren’t fine because I had a scar. He made me feel so bad. I lost confidence. Now my legs are being called beautiful,” she wrote.

The reality star continued, “this life ehn! When he is not for you, he is truly not for you. There will always be someone who is ready to massage those scars.”

Uriel is a renowned advocate for body positivity.

She came into limelight in 2017 when she participated in the rebooted Big Brother Naija edition.
