They will stand with you when the rest of the world leaves you.

Former Nairobi Governor Mike Sonko's daughters have bent over backwards to put a smile on his face ever since tragedy became his second name.

Even his youngest girl Sandra stepped up and became his pillar of support.

On Wednesday, March 3, the politician turned a year older and his princesses wanted to remind him of deep their love for their dad runs.

So they made a collage of all their precious moments with Sonko then posted them on Instagram.

All that was followed by wonderful messages that described the kind of father and husband Sonko is.

To his baby girls, he will always be Papa, their first love and forever protector.

Sandra promised to love her dad through thick and thin and praised him for being a standup guy.

"Papa you've given me so many invaluable things in life and I will always be grateful for them. May your special day bring you plenty of wonderful surprises," Sandra wrote.
