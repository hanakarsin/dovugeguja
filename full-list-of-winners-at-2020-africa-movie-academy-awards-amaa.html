Full List Of Winners At 2020 Africa Movie Academy Awards (AMAA)

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, May 18, 2024

Winners have emerged in the 2020 edition of the Africa Movie Academy Awards (AMAA).

The event held virtually on Sunday 20th December due to the global COVID-19 pandemic which prevented gathering in large numbers.

It was a good outing for ‘The Milkmaid‘, a film by Nigeria producer, Desmond Ovbiagele as the film won in five out of the eight nominations it got for the 2020 AMAA.

Below is the full list of nominations and eventual winners at the award.
