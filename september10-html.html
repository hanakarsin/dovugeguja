September 10 Horoscope and Zodiac

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, April 7, 2024

The Virgo's planetary ruler is Mercury, but as you were born in the second Decan, or part, of the sign, you also experience Saturn's planetary powers. Mercury's mysterious power can be witnessed in your sharp communication skills and intellect. In the same light, Saturn's power can be seen in your patience, perseverance and discipline. These two planet's combine to make you far more success driven than the other Virgo Decans. You are also the most natural leaders. While your gifts are obvious, you may be surprised to find that you are a bit of the perfectionist. You can easily become frustrated with others and yourself in times where your accomplishments fall short of ideal. Keep your pragmatism when dealing with the expectations you place on yourself and loved ones. Embrace your sensitivity with people, as it plays a key role in your happiness.
