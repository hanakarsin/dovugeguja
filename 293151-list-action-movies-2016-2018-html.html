List of the best action movies 2016 to 2018

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, February 4, 2024

Are you a lover of action? If yes is your answer, you are in the right place. Between the years 2016 and 2018 there has been a release after release of action filled movies from the 2016 hit movie My Bodyguard to the legendary Black Panther of 2018. Throughout the three consecutive years, the film industry has produced awesome stuff that has left all action movie fans wanting more. Below is a list of the best action movies 2016 to 2018.

Between the years 2016 and 2018, the theatres have been flocking with the lovers of action movies. The number of action movies that have been released in the three years is very interesting to watch. The most tantalizing part is that these action movies integrate thrilling scenes, crime, drama, and adventure. If you are looking forward to enjoying your weekend away from the hustle and bustle of your everyday weekly routine, action movies are the way to go. Here is a list of some of the best releases for the past three years.

The evacuation takes place at the seaside town of French, hence the title of the movie. Nolan successfully creates the view of this historic event; World War II, in all dimensions. If you are a crazy fan of action thrillers then this is one of the best action movies you should consider watching Dunkirk.
