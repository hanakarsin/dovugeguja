Fast Rising Nollywood Actress Is Dead

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

A popular Nollywood makeup artist and actress Tosin Abeniade Badmus is dead.

According to Yorubamoviegist.com, the cause of her death is still unknown. Badmus was reported to have been ill for a while before she died in the early hours of Monday, November 30.

[article_adwert]

The young Badmus was a makeup artist in the Yoruba genre of Nollywood.
