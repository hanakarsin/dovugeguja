APC Sets Up 86-Member Campaign Council For 2022 Osun Governorship Election (Full List)

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, March 18, 2024

The All Progressives Congress (APC) has set up an 86-member campaign council to lead the party’s campaign strategy and activities for the 2022 governorship election in Osun State.

This was disclosed in a statement on Tuesday signed by the party’s National Chairman, Senator Abdullahi Adamu and its National Secretary, Senator Iyiola Omisore.

The council made up of strategic members of the party, is headed by the Lagos State Governor, Babajide Sanwo-Olu as chairman while Mallam Aliyu Ahmed will serve as the secretary.

The campaign council will be inaugurated on Thursday, June 23 by 2 pm at the APC National Headquarters in Abuja, Naija News reports.

“NOTICE: The inauguration of the All Progressives Congress (APC) National Campaign Council for the Osun State Governorship Election will be held on Thursday, 23rd of June, 2022 by 2:00pm at the APC National Headquarters, Abuja,” the party tweeted on its official Twitter handle.
