- A follower curiously asked whether the programme is about to come to an end

- The critic argued that the twists and turns were quite tiring

A lot of her followers were thrilled by the video and expressed their urge to view the full episode as soon as it was out.

To them, all the suspense, the surprises, the drama and twists are worthy and welcomed.

Then one daredevil decided to be the devil's advocate.

The opinionated follower wondered when the show will come to an end and claimed the twists and turns were becoming tiring.

Lulu confidently rebutted with a simple, "it will not end anytime soon."

As previously highlighted by TUKO.co.ke, the executive producers of the show and founders of Jiffy Pictures, Lulu and Rashid , organised a four-day tour of Mombasa and Kilifi for the fans to meet their stars.

After the tour was over, on the night of Sunday, January 31, the couple had something planned for their stars to enjoy themselves and celebrate their successful trip.
