Patty Walters - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Pop singer best known as the lead vocalist of the band As It Is. He is joined by fellow members Andy Westhead, Patrick Foley, Ben Bissington, and Alistair Testo. 

Before Fame

He is a popular YouTuber who uploaded his first video, a cover of Joshua Radin's song "Winter," on October 20th, 2009. 

Trivia

He joined his fellow band members to form As It Is in May of 2012. In 2014, he announced on YouTube that his band was signed to Fearless Records. 

Family Life

He has been in a relationship with Dottie Martin since June of 2013; they briefly broke up in 2018. The couple got engaged in mid-2020 and were married in November 2021. 

Associated With

He was featured in a music video by Luke Cutforth.
