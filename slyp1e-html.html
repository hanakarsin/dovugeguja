SLYP1E - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, April 11, 2024

Short film content creator on YouTube who uses Five Nights at Freddy's plushies for his videos. He provides voices for the characters as he moves them around. His channel has earned 1.1 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

He started his channel in November of 2016 with a video called "FNAF Plush Episode 1: The Juice Bar." 

Trivia

His first videos to surpass 1 million views were "Stay Calm FNAF Plush Music Video" and "FNAF Plush Movie (EXCLUSIVE)." Another of his most popular videos, focusing on a storyline about parts and service, has garnered over 13 million views. 

Family Life

He was born in Portland, Oregon. He introduced his pet siamese cat, Lorelei on his instagram page in August 2023.

Associated With

He and Johnny Albert are known for their Five Nights at Freddy's plush toy series videos.
