Sharon Otieno's Mother Says She warned Her Against Dating Governor Obado

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, July 8, 2024
  • Late Sharon Otieno's mother Melida Auma has revealed she had constantly warned her daughter against her affair with Governor Okoth Obado
  • Despite her mother's advice, Sharon still pursued the relationship with the embattled county chief
  • She died in cold blood in September 2018

The mother of the late Sharon Otieno has revealed she had warned her daughter against engaging Migori governor Okoth Obado in an intimate affair.

Melida Auma stated that she was aware of her daughter's engagement with the governor and had advised her against it but to no avail.

Speaking to Radio Jambo, Auma said her daughter was adamant to let go of the relationship and thus forged ahead with it.

"The relationship did not sit well with me. I even told her not to go ahead with it, but she was a grown up. Maybe it was under peer influence she pursued it," she said.

Auma further denied the claims that her daughter's decision to pursue the affair with the governor was triggered by the lack of a fatherly figure in her life.

The mother said that she has always been a disciplinarian alongside Sharon's father and thus the decision by their daughter to date an elderly person was purely out of peer pressure.

"If there was anything bad I would tell her father. He knows me very well, I am a known disciplinarian at home," she said.

Sharon died in September 2018 in cold blood.

Affair with Obado

It was alleged the then Rongo University student was in a relationship with the embattled Obado who has since been facing charges related to murder.

The goner enjoyed all the benefits and advantages that came along with being the governor's mistress until she became pregnant.

When she informed him of the matter, reports say, Obado slowly started detaching himself from her.

The university student noted the cold treatment and did all that she could to gain the attention and support of her said lover.

Sharon's photos of her baby bump and even the ultrasound pictures sent to the governor got no response until her murder.

Obado in corridors of justice

The governor was arrested after the incident and spent months in remand before he was released on a KSh 5 million bail.

The case is still pending in court and efforts by Obado to stop the prosecution from proceeding with the matter over COVID-19 protocols were thwarted the last time the matter came up for hearing.

The court rejected the plea by the governor, a main suspect in the matter.
