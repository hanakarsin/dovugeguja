'Davido Should Do A Major Deliverance'

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Nigerians on social media have expressed concern for singer, Davido, after losing his personal Photographer Fortune Ateumunname, few months after the death of Obama DMW.

Naija News reports that  Davido‘s photographer, Fortunate Umurname Peter, 24, who was born and raised in Bariga reportedly died on September 21st.

According to reports, Fortune drowned at a location in Lagos on Tuesday evening during a shoot. 

Naija News understands that Fortune Ateumunnam’s death is coming few months after the death of Obama DMW.

Reacting to the news of his death, Nigerians via social media questioned why death trails the DMW camp, whilst consoling the singer over the death of his photographer.

They also urged the singer to go for a major deliverance to quell the raising deaths in his camp.
