2023: Chatham House Trying To Force Sick, Delinquent Tinubu On Nigerians

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, February 8, 2024

The presidential candidate of the African Action Congress (AAC), Omoyele Sowore has hit out against the international organization known as Chatham House, accusing it of attempting to force a candidate on Nigerians in 2023.

Sowore alleged that the Royal Institute of International Affairs better known as Chatham House is trying to force the candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu on Nigerians.

The media publisher added that it was the same way the UK-based outfit packaged President Muhammadu Buhari and forced him on Nigerians in 2015.

He however rejected the choice of Tinubu, describing the former Lagos State Governor as a derelict, sick, and delinquent.

He wrote: “Pay-Per-Play: @ChathamHouse working to impose a derelict, sick and delinquent @Asiwaju
Ahmed Tinubu on Nigeria. They did the same for Muhammadu Buhari!

“They probably wrote his speech too, because once you start to hear “energy security“ know that someone is hailing Britain and America by their middle name. #WeCantContinueLikeThis.”

My Records Are True And Intact

The presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu, has agreed to make himself available for a Deoxyribonucleic Acid (DNA) test to confirm his lineage.

Naija News reports that the former Governor of Lagos State made the bold move on Monday during his presentation at Chatham House in London, the United Kingdom, where he spoke on his plans for Nigeria and foreign policy imperatives ahead of the 2023 general elections.

Tinubu insisted that he remains a proper member of the Tinubu family, faulting his counterparts from other political parties who have been criticizing his identity at any opportunity.

He insisted that his birth, academic, and professional records are consistent, adding that his critics are only wasting their time and money.
