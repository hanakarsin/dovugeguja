The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip Season 3 Cast Posted Their First Vacation Photo

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, July 18, 2024

'The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip' is getting a Season 3, and the cast began filming in July 2022. Find out the details on the cast!

While each Real Housewives show follows a group of friends or frenemies who all live in the same city or region, the Peacock original The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip (RHUGT) broke the mold by bringing stars together from different franchises for epic vacations.

After reuniting some current and former fan-favorite housewives in Turks and Caicos in Season 1, the series landed another season, titled Ex-Wives Club. The new season followed several former cast members spending time at Real Housewives of New York alum Dorinda Medley’s home, Bluestone Manor. The explosive seven-episode season kept viewers coming back and eager to watch Season 3.

Fortunately, fans didn’t have to wait long for details on RHUGT’s future. The show has been renewed for a third season and began filming with its new cast in July 2022. RHUGT Season 3 follows the same format as the first season by adding current and past cast members. We’ve got the details on the RHUGT cast before the show releases Season 3!

Heather Gay

The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City star Heather joins RHUGT Season 3. Fortunately for her fans, though, Heather has no plans to leave RHOSLC and become part of the ex-wives club. The Bad Mormon author will likely dive more into her decision to leave the Mormon faith and her family’s issues. However, there’s no word if she’ll discuss her pal Jen Shah’s guilty plea for wire fraud.

Whitney Rose

Heather’s cousin and RHOSLC cast member Whitney is also a part of the RHUGT Season 3 cast. Whitney joined RHOSLC in Season 1 with her husband Justin and children Bobbie and Brooks.

Gizelle Bryant

The Real Housewives of Potomac’s OG, Gizelle, joined RHUGT Season 3 and showed plenty of bawdy in the cast’s photo in Thailand. After Peacock and Bravo released the snap, Gizelle reposted it on Instagram and promised, "THAILAND will never be the same!”

Alexia Echevarria

Alexia from The Real Housewives of Miami brings the South Beach heat to Thailand! Although her show recently returned after an eight-year hiatus, Alexia is ready to show what the Miami girls have to offer.

“Did someone say Ultimate Girls Trip?!” Alexia wrote on Instagram under the cast’s photo.

Candiace Dillard-Bassett

Candiace will also appear on RHUGT Season 3. The “Drive Back” songstress’ role gig on the Bravo spinoff comes after she hinted at leaving The Real Housewives of Potomac on Twitter. Candiace hasn’t confirmed if she’s leaving RHOP, but it looks like she’s ready for some fun in the meantime!

Porsha Williams

Porsha may be off of The Real Housewives of Atlanta, but she is still on Bravo execs’ minds. In July 2022, People announced that Porsha would replace RHONY alum Tinsley Mortimer. Tinsley reportedly quit RHUGT for “personal reasons,” causing Porsha to take her spot. The longtime peach holder left RHOA in September 2021 before appearing on her spinoff, Porsha’s Family Matters.

Leah McSweeney

Tinsley’s RHUGT departure means Leah will be without her Bravo best friend this season. Nonetheless, the RHONY star is ready to take on Thailand with a new crowd of housewives. Leah’s time on the spinoff comes after she shared with the We Should Talk podcast that she didn’t see herself returning to RHONY amid its Season 14 shakeup. During the interview, she declared, “I think I'm not coming back. It's just the better way to think about it."

Marysol Patton

Marysol will team up with her good friend Alexia to represent the Miami girls. While Marysol didn’t hold a mojito when the show returned in Season 4, the OG’s fans still have her in high regard. The former PR executive also doesn’t mind, as she told Entertainment Tonight in February 2022, “just give me the paycheck! I brought my own mojito.”

You can stream The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip on Peacock.
