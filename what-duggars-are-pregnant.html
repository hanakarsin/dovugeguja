These Are the Duggars Who Are 'Counting On' by Expanding Their Families

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, March 28, 2024

What Duggars are pregnant? A comprehensive list of the Duggar kids who will be expanding their very own families, including the ones on 'Counting On.'

At basically any given moment, odds are that at least one of the Duggar children is expecting a baby. With 19 kidsin the Duggar family who are all seemingly interested in having big families themselves, the size of the clan has grown significantly since their TLC show 19 Kids & Counting went off the air in 2015 and the spinoff Counting On began.

Eleven of the Duggar kids are married, and they most began expanding their own nuclear families shortly after walking down the aisle. What Duggars are pregnant? We know it's hard to keep track, so we did the work for you.

These are the Duggars who are pregnant, have children, or are married.

Joy-Anna and Austin Forsyth

Joy-Anna and Austin Forsyth tied the knot in May 2017 before having their first child, Gideon, in May 2018. The Counting On star revealed in July 2019 that she miscarried a baby girl at 20 weeks along. (She and Austin named her Annabell Elise.)

Joy and Austin's second child, a daughter they named Evelyn, was born on Aug. 21, 2020.

In October 2022, the couple revealed they were expecting baby No. 3 in March 2023.

Jeremiah and Hannah Duggar

Jeremiah Duggar and Hannah Wissmann announced their engagement in January 2022. They went on to say "I do" on March 27, 2022. Just like many Duggar siblings before him, a baby announcement soon followed their wedding.

In late August 2022, Jer and Hannah revealed via Instagram that they were expecting their first child. They haven't yet shared a due date, but if Hannah was 12 weeks along when they announced, she would be due sometime in mid March 2023.

John David and Abbie Duggar

At the ripe old age of 28, John David finally walked down the aisle in November of 2018 with Abbie Grace Burnett. The couple announced that they were expecting their first child together in August of 2019, and they later did a gender reveal and learned that they were having a baby girl. On Jan. 8, 2020, John David and Abbie welcomed little Grace Annette. And in September 2022, Abbie delivered a baby boy named Charlie.

Jill and Derick Dillard

Jill and Derick Dillard have been married since June 2014. They've gone on to welcome two sons: Israel (born on April 6, 2015) and Samuel (born on July 8, 2017). In February 2022, Jill and Derick announced they were expecting baby number three, another boy. They went on to welcome son Frederick on July 7, 2022.

Josh and Anna Duggar

Josh Duggar is most notably known now for being the reason why 19 Kids and Counting got canceled in the first place, after it was revealed that he had molested several of his sisters, and was active on the cheating website Ashley Madison. But, wife Anna stayed with Josh, and they kept having children. (Duggar is currently awaiting sentencing following two guilty verdicts in his child pornography trial.)

Currently, they have seven kids: Mackynzie, Michael, Marcus, Meredith, Mason, Maryella, and Madyson.

Jessa and Ben Seewald

To round out the list of Duggars who had kids in 2019, Jessa and Ben were the first of the year to add another baby to their family. Already parents to sons, Spurgeon and Henry, Jessa and Ben were in for a change when they had their first daughter. Ivy Jane Seewald was born on May 26, 2019. They went on to welcome a fourth child, Fern Elliana, on July 18, 2021.

Joseph and Kendra Duggar

Joseph and Kendra had their first child, son Garrett, less than a year after their 2017 nuptials. Kendra's second pregnancy was announced in April of 2019, and they welcomed daughter Addison Renee in early November 2019.

Keeping their child-spacing consistent, Joe and Kendra revealed in August 2019 that they were expecting baby No. 3. The two welcomed daughter Brooklyn Praise on Feb. 19, 2021.

Jinger and Jeremy Vuolo

On May 28, 2020, the Counting On couple announced they were expecting baby No. 2 (another girl!) after Jinger suffered a miscarriage in fall 2019. They welcomed their second child, Evangeline Jo, on Nov. 22, 2020.

Josiah and Lauren Duggar

After getting married in June of 2018, the couple wasted no time in trying to expand their family. They soon announced that they were expecting, but Lauren had a miscarriage shortly thereafter. Several months later, the couple announced that they were having their rainbow baby, and that it was a girl. In November 2019, Lauren and Josiah welcomed daughter Bella Milagro, whose name translates to "Beautiful Miracle." Pass us the tissues. 

Justin and Claire Duggar

Justin Duggar and Claire Spivey tied the knot on Feb. 26, 2021. Although they have not yet made a pregnancy announcement, Counting On fans think it's only a matter of time before they do.

Jed and Katey Duggar

After tying the knot on April 3, 2021, Jed and Katey announced in September that they were expecting their first child. They went on to welcome a baby boy they named Truett Oliver Duggar on May 2, 2022.

Amy Duggar King and Dillon King

Jim Bob's niece Amy was the definite wild child of the Duggars, as she had a more conventional dating process with husband Dillon than the formal courting her cousins go through. After getting married in 2015, she and Dillon waited several years (which is also rare for a Duggar) before having a baby. Amy gave birth to son Daxton Ryan King in October 2019.

If you lost count, that means two Duggar couples are currently pregnant (at least that we know of). Many have grown up with the Duggars, and it's evident that the next generation is well on its way.
