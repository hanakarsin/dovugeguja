Andre Nguyen - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Burgeoning portrait photographer who has built an audience on Instagram by photographing internet stars like Sierra Furtado, Lele Pons, and Ray Diaz, amongst others. He has amassed over 240,000 followers.

Before Fame

After graduating from Aliso Niguel High School, he went on to study at the University of California, Santa Barbara. 

Trivia

In addition to his yourfriendandre Instagram account, he has a YouTube channel of the same name. 

Family Life

He grew up in Aliso Viejo with his parents and two younger brothers. 

Associated With

Some of the stars featured in his stunning photographs include Carrington Durham, Casimere Jollette, and Ashley Campbell.
