The former reality TV star urged women to stop hating and rejoice with one another.

In her own words:

''Women, we are our worst enemies and that why I keep my circle small. Let's learn to celebrate each other,'' she wrote on Instagram.

According to Faith, women don't stand by each other.

She has also advised them to passionately embrace each other cheekily noting that women can dislike each other even if they are married to one man.

''Let's not put each other down; we might even be sharing the same husbands and we hate each other,'' added Faith.

Faith's kicker that women are their own enemies is a subject that many have agreed with in the past.

Most of the people who commented on the post agreed with Faith.
