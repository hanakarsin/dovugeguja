Lance Reddick - Trivia, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, February 16, 2024

Most well known for starring in the critically loved series The Wire as Cedric Daniels and also for his roles in Fringe and Oz. He was recognized for his role as Charon in the popular action movie franchise John Wick. He amassed more than 100 different acting roles throughout his prolific career. 

Before Fame

He attended a summer music program that taught music theory and composition and later attended the Yale School of Drama. 

Trivia

He is a co-producer and actor in the YouTube web series, DR0NE. He passed away suddenly at the age of 60. 

Family Life

Married at a young age, his daughter was born while he was still working as a struggling musician. 

Associated With

He was featured in the music video for the song "03 Bonnie & Clyde" with Jay-Z and Beyonce.
