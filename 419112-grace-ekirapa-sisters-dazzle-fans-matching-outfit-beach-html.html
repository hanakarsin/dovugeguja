Grace Ekirapa, Sisters Dazzle Fans with Matching Outfit at the Beach

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Monday, February 26, 2024
  • Media personality Grace Ekirapa and her sisters wowed netizens with their matching beachwear outfits
  • The three donned tiny denim shorts, white laced kimonos and white vests while spending time at the beach
  • Ekirapa is on a vacation at the coast with her family and her hubby Pascal Tokodi recently teased her online that she will be back in the city

TV show host Grace Ekirapa has amused fans after sharing delectable photos of herself and two sisters in matching outfits while spending time at the beach.

Grace Ekirapa and her sisters

The celebrity shared the photos on her Instastory on Instagram as they enjoyed time together at the coast.

In the photos, the three were dressed in tiny denim shorts, white vests and white laced kimonos.

The three have been doing things together and sharing their fun activities online with fans enjoying their sisterhood relationship.

Ekirapa, who is married to renowned actor Pascal Tokodi teased fans after sharing a post online flaunting her long light skinned legs.

She posted a snap enjoying the beautiful coastal scenery with a sunny climate, unlike Nairobi's cold weather.

The beauty looked stunning in a beach dress, tiny shorts and a hat as she overlooked the ocean.

With a nice hat and smile on her face, the lass captioned her post, "Baridi for who" which caught the attention of her celebrity hubby.

Tokodi then aimed a dig at her, which kick-started a lovely-dovey chat that excited their fans.
