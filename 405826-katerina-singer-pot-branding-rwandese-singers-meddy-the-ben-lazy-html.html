The musician was captured in a leaked audio complaining about people who dare compare him to the two great lyricists.

To him, Meddy and The Ben only release one song per year.

“First of all, they are lazy. They are really lazy. They release one song in a year but I release hit songs every now and then. You cannot find any similarity between me and those guys,” the embittered man said.

According to Melodie, he is way more hardworking than the two US-based crooners famous for their songs Habibi and Slowly.

A couple of people suggested that the singer get off his high horse and learn how to appreciate his fellow celebrities’ talent.

To most guys who got hold of the audio, Melodie is far behind and has a lot to learn from the two Rwandese giants.
