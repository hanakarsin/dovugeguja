Female Fan Gets Intimate With Singer Flavour On Stage (Video)

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, May 20, 2024

Nigerian singer, Chinedu Okoli, popularly known as Flavour, has shared a video of his intimate moment with a female fan on stage.

Naija News reports that Flavour, who is currently on a music tour in North America had a fun time with a white female fan while performing on stage.

In the video shared on his Instagram page, the singer was been caressed from his chest to his waist which got the audience screaming at the top of their voices.

While singing, Flavour softly said, ‘Oyinbo wants it with the African man oo’ and later gave the female fan a warm hug before she exited the stage.

Frank Edwards Denies Engagement To Deborah Enenche

Meanwhile, renowned Nigerian gospel singer, Frank Edwards, has taken to his Instagram page to end speculations that he got Deborah Paul Enenche engaged.

Naija News understands that there have been speculations that the Rocktown Records Label Chief Executive Officer (CEO) engaged the daughter of the Senior Pastor of the Dunamis International Gospel Center (DIGC), Dr Paul Enenche, after the young lady took to social media to share pictures of her engagement ring.

Deborah refused to disclose the identity of her lover. However, netizens were quick to link her up with Frank Edwards after a photo she took with the 33-year-old singer surfaced on the internet.

Reacting, however, to the speculations, Frank Edwards took to his Instagram handle on Thursday to declare that the news is not true.
