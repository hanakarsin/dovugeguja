Rydel Funk - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, February 23, 2024

Pop singer who became best known as the only female member of the pop band R5, alongside her brothers Ross, Riker, Rocky and Ryland. The band has also featured Ellington Ratliff. Their 2013 EP Ready, Set, Rock yielded the hit song "Loud." 

Before Fame

She took keyboard and tambourine lessons and was also a dancer. 

Trivia

She began performing with her siblings in R5 in 2009 and she opened the LOUD tour in March 2013. She launched her own online boutique RYDEL in February 2018. 

Family Life

Her parents, Stormie and Mark Lynch, raised her alongside her four brothers: Ross, Riker, Rocky, and Ryland. She began dating bandmate Ellington in 2013 and later proposed to him in December 2017; however, they'd ultimately split up in 2018. Subsequently, she began dating Capron Funk in 2019. She and Capron Funk got engaged in 2020 and the couple wed on September 12, 2020. In April 2021, they welcomed their first child together, a boy named Super. The family shares their lives on the YouTube channel The Funks. The couple announced they are expecting a baby girl. 

Associated With

She sang "Here Comes Forever" with her younger brother Ross Lynch.
